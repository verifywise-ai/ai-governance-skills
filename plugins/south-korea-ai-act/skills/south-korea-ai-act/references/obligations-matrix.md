# South Korea AI Basic Act — Obligations matrix

## Obligations by role

The AI Basic Act assigns differentiated obligations to AI developers, deployers, and users. The table below maps each obligation to the applicable role and indicates whether it applies to all AI systems or only to high-risk AI systems.

### Core obligations

| Obligation | AI Developer | AI Deployer | AI User | Scope | Description |
|-----------|-------------|------------|---------|-------|-------------|
| Impact assessment | ✅ Must conduct before deployment | ✅ Must review and validate | — | High-risk only | Assess potential impacts on life, safety, and fundamental rights before deploying high-risk AI |
| Transparency and disclosure | ✅ Must enable disclosure mechanisms | ✅ Must disclose AI use to affected persons | ✅ Must not conceal AI involvement | All AI systems | Affected individuals must be informed that AI is being used in decisions or interactions |
| Human oversight | ✅ Must design systems to allow human oversight | ✅ Must implement human oversight procedures | — | High-risk only | High-risk AI systems must be designed and operated to allow meaningful human intervention |
| Safety measures | ✅ Must implement safety-by-design | ✅ Must maintain safety during operation | — | High-risk only | High-risk AI systems must incorporate technical and organizational safety measures |
| Record-keeping | ✅ Must maintain development and testing records | ✅ Must maintain operational records | — | High-risk only | Records of AI system development, testing, and operation must be maintained |
| Incident reporting | ✅ Must report significant incidents to MSIT | ✅ Must report significant incidents to MSIT | — | High-risk only | Significant incidents involving high-risk AI must be reported to the Ministry of Science and ICT |
| AI ethics compliance | ✅ Must comply | ✅ Must comply | ✅ Must comply | All AI systems | All entities must adhere to national AI ethics principles (human dignity, fairness, transparency, safety, accountability) |
| Deepfake labeling | ✅ Must provide labeling capability | ✅ Must ensure labels are applied | ✅ Must not remove labels | All AI-generated content | AI-generated synthetic content, including deepfakes, must be clearly labeled |
| Right to explanation | ✅ Must design systems to enable explanations | ✅ Must provide explanations upon request | — | Significant automated decisions | Individuals affected by significant automated decisions may request an explanation |
| Data governance | ✅ Must ensure training data quality and fairness | — | — | All AI systems | Developers must ensure training data is of sufficient quality and does not embed discriminatory bias |

### Developer-specific obligations

| Obligation | Description | Applicable AI systems |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|
| Safety-by-design | Incorporate safety measures from the design phase | All AI systems, enhanced for high-risk |
| Training data quality | Ensure training, validation, and test data meet quality standards | All AI systems |
| Bias prevention | Assess and mitigate discriminatory bias in AI system design and data | All AI systems |
| Impact assessment execution | Conduct and document impact assessment before deployment | High-risk AI systems |
| Human oversight design | Design systems to enable effective human oversight and intervention | High-risk AI systems |
| Technical documentation | Prepare and maintain documentation of system design, capabilities, and limitations | High-risk AI systems |
| Labeling infrastructure | Provide mechanisms for labeling AI-generated content | AI systems generating content |

### Deployer-specific obligations

| Obligation | Description | Applicable AI systems |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|
| AI use disclosure | Inform affected persons that AI is being used in decisions or interactions | All AI systems |
| Impact assessment review | Review and validate the developer's impact assessment before deployment | High-risk AI systems |
| Human oversight implementation | Establish and maintain human oversight procedures during operation | High-risk AI systems |
| Explanation provision | Provide explanations of significant automated decisions upon individual request | AI systems making significant decisions |
| Incident reporting | Report significant AI-related incidents to MSIT | High-risk AI systems |
| Operational safety | Maintain safety measures during AI system operation and use | High-risk AI systems |
| Deepfake label enforcement | Ensure AI-generated content carries appropriate labels | AI systems generating content |
| Record-keeping | Maintain records of AI system use, decisions, and incidents | High-risk AI systems |

### User-specific obligations

| Obligation | Description | Applicable AI systems |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|
| Ethics compliance | Adhere to national AI ethics principles when using AI system outputs | All AI systems |
| Non-concealment | Do not conceal AI involvement when using AI-generated outputs in contexts where disclosure is required | All AI systems |
| Label preservation | Do not remove or obscure AI-generated content labels | AI-generated content |
